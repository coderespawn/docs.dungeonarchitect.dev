Getting Started

Welcome to Dungeon Architect, a powerful plugin for creating intricate and immersive dungeons in your games.

In this section, we'll guide you through the initial steps to quickly start building your first dungeon and designing custom themes. You'll discover the wide range of possibilities Dungeon Architect offers, enabling you to unleash your creativity and craft unique gaming experiences.

We'll also have a look at the Launch Pad, our built-in sample browser. It provides access to a variety of pre-made dungeon samples, showcasing different styles and techniques. These samples serve as valuable references and a good starting point for your own dungeon designs.
